20th Convocation Instructions to students

INSTRUCTIONS

1.

Please read the instructions carefully. Application will be incomplete if the mandatory fields are not filled.

2. Incomplete form will not be accepted & will not be processed.

3.

Please add donotreply_exams@siu.edu.in to your safe sender list to avoid registration confirmation mail from going to your SPAM/JUNK folder.

4.

Avoid using corporate email id / hotmail email id due to delivery issues of SIU registration confirmation email.

5.

Degree / Diploma will not be issued if any fees are pending.

6.

Alumnus who wish to obtain their Degree / Diploma by post will have to provide detailed address in the registration form.

7.

You will need to pay the postal charges as per the details given below if your Degree/ Diploma was not delivered due to incomplete address provided by you / unavailability on your part.
• Rs 250/- within India
• Rs.1750/- outside India (International Speed Post)

8.

Alumnus who wish to collect their Degree / Diploma personally will have to carry government ID proof.
